01. About me

Hi! I’m Annie Loxton, and I enjoy creating cool things! I first became interested in Web Development during my studies where we learned basic HTML and CSS. Being able to create your own websites is such a great feeling!

After working as a UI/UX designer for a few years, I decided it was time to revisit Web Development, since all the projects that I worked on eventually get handed over to developers, and it would be such a bonus for me to understand everything that goes into the development process.

I would love to be able to add development into my skillset, and sell it alongside my website designs. Even if I don’t end up doing that, it will make me a better designer to understand how websites get developed.
